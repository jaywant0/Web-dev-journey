Gulab jamun

Incredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Mix the dough ingredients together.
  2. Boil the water and sugar together to make the syrup.
  3. Add cardamom and rose water to the cooled syrup.
  4. Fry the dough balls.
  5. Add the fried dough balls to the syrup.
  6. Refrigerate for at least an hour.
  7. Serve warm.

Pohe

Incredients

  • Flattened rice (poha)
  • Cumin seeds, mustard seeds, coriander powder
  • Onion, green chilies, potato

Recipe

  1. De-husking rice grains
  2. Soaking rice in hot water
  3. Parboiling (partially cooking) the rice
  4. Flattening the rice into flakes using rollers
